Add `podkit doctor --repair artwork-reset` to clear all artwork from an iPod without needing a source collection. This is a fast alternative to a full rebuild — useful when your source collection isn't available or you just want to clear corrupted artwork quickly.

Rename `--repair artwork-integrity` to `--repair artwork-rebuild` to better describe what the repair does. The old name no longer works.
